# Introduction

## What is Nexora?

Nexora is a distributed node network where users run lightweight CLI-based nodes on their machines — laptops, VPS, or even Android devices — and earn points based on their activity and uptime.

It is not a mining platform. There is no heavy computation, no GPU required, and no blockchain consensus to participate in. Nexora is built around a **Proof-of-Activity** model: if your node is online, contributing, and behaving honestly, you earn rewards.

---

## Vision

To build a fair, accessible, and transparent distributed network where anyone with a device and an internet connection can participate and be rewarded — without barriers, without waste.

---

## Mission

- Make distributed infrastructure participation accessible to everyone
- Reward honest, consistent contribution over raw computational power
- Build a trustworthy system with transparent rules and anti-abuse mechanisms
- Grow a community-driven network that scales with its participants

---

## The Problem Nexora Solves

Most distributed networks today have one or more of the following issues:

- **High entry barrier** — require expensive hardware or technical expertise
- **Energy waste** — rely on proof-of-work models that burn electricity for no productive output
- **Unfair reward distribution** — favor early adopters or large operators disproportionately
- **No anti-abuse layer** — easy to game with bots or fake nodes

Nexora addresses all of these by focusing on lightweight participation, uptime-based rewards, and a built-in anti-cheat system that keeps the network honest.
